Meet Vita

Vita is your personal health intelligence. A strict coach who watches your recovery, tracks your workouts, monitors your sleep, and ensures you don't burn out or slack off.

No excuses. No negotiations. Vita sees the data and tells you the truth. "Recovery at 87%. Ready to push today." or "Sleep debt: 2.3 hours. Early night tonight."

Vita's Personality

  • Strict Coach: No excuses accepted
  • Data-Driven: Facts over feelings
  • Protective: Prevents burnout
  • Motivating: Celebrates wins
  • Predictive: Sees patterns before you do

What Vita Tracks

Every metric that matters for optimal physical performance.

Recovery & Strain

WHOOP recovery score, strain, HRV (heart rate variability), resting heart rate. Know exactly how hard you can push today.

Workouts

Frequency, intensity, type, duration. Automatically logged from WHOOP or Apple Watch. No manual entry needed.

Sleep

Total sleep, deep sleep, REM, sleep debt. Vita tracks quality and correlates it with next-day performance.

Daily Activity

Steps, active calories, sedentary time. Vita notices if you've been sitting too long.

Nutrition

Integration with Cal AI. Calorie intake, macros, meal timing. Correlated with performance and energy.

Trends

7-day, 30-day, 90-day trends. Vita identifies patterns: "Workout consistency declining for 2 weeks."
