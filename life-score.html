What is Life Score?

Your Life Score is a single metric (0-100) that represents the overall health of your life across all 6 spheres.

It's not an average. It's a weighted aggregate calculated by Oracle based on:

  • Individual sphere performance
  • Sphere interdependencies
  • Your personal priorities
  • Historical trends
  • Cross-sphere correlations
  • Velocity of change

Life Score 79

How Oracle Calculates Your Score

Not a simple average. A sophisticated algorithm that sees the whole.

1. Base Scores

Each sphere gets a score (0-100) based on its own metrics. Vita scores Health. Argentum scores Finance. And so on.

2. Weighted Importance

You can weight spheres differently. Entrepreneur? Business might be 30%. New parent? Social/Health might be 40%.

3. Correlations

Oracle discovers connections. If Health ↑ correlates with Business ↑, that synergy boosts your score.

4. Velocity

A sphere moving up? Bonus. Stagnant for weeks? Penalty. Direction matters as much as position.

5. Balance

91 in Business but 42 in Health? That's unsustainable. Oracle penalizes extreme imbalance.

6. Your Goals

Your goals inform priority. 56 goals across spheres tell Oracle what matters to you.

Understanding Your Score

0-40: Crisis

Multiple spheres failing. Immediate intervention needed. Oracle triggers emergency mode with aggressive nudges.

41-60: Struggling

Some areas weak. Unsustainable patterns. Oracle identifies the weakest sphere and prioritizes fixes.

61-75: Functional

Baseline okay. Room for growth. Oracle pushes you toward optimization in specific areas.

76-85: Thriving

Most spheres healthy. Good balance. Oracle helps maintain and suggests incremental improvements.

86-95: Excellent

All spheres strong. Sustainable patterns. Oracle focuses on maintaining excellence and preventing regression.

96-100: Peak

Rare. All spheres optimal. Synergies strong. Oracle helps you stay at peak without burning out.
